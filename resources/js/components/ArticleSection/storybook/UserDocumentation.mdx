# Article Section

The **Article Section** block displays a group of articles with a section heading and optional carousel layout.

Articles can be pulled from **internal posts**, an **external API**, or **added manually**, allowing you to feature news, stories, or announcements in a structured section.

---

## When to Use This Block

Use the Article Section when:

- You want to highlight a group of related articles
- You need a featured news section on a page
- You want articles displayed in a **carousel or grouped layout**

### Example uses:

- Homepage news highlights
- Department news sections
- Featured stories
- Academic news or faculty accomplishments

## Block Settings

### Display Posts Method

Choose how articles will be added to the section.

Options include:

- **Internal Posts** – Display posts from within the site.
- **External API** – Pull articles from an external feed.
- **Add Posts Manually** – Create custom article items.

---

### Carousel

If enabled, articles will display in a **horizontal carousel** that visitors can scroll through.

If disabled, articles will display in a **standard layout**.

---

## Section Content

### Context

Add the section’s introductory content, such as:

- Title
- Description
- Link or call-to-action

This appears **above the list of articles**.

---

## Adding Items Manually

When **Add Posts Manually** is selected, you can create custom article items.

Each item includes the following fields:

### Image

Upload or select an image to represent the article.

---

### Context

Add supporting content such as:

- Title
- Description
- Link

---

## External API Settings

When **External API** is selected, additional options appear.

### API

Choose which external feed to display.

Examples include:

- Academic News
- Faculty Accomplishments

---

## Internal Post Settings

When **Internal Posts** is selected, the section can automatically display posts from the site.

### Render Posts

If enabled, posts will be automatically pulled into the section.

---

### Render Posts Category

Choose which post category should be displayed.

---

## Tips for Best Results

- Use this block for **featured article sections** on major pages.
- Add a clear **section title and description** using the Context field.
- Enable **Carousel** when displaying many articles in a limited space.
- Use **Manual items** when you want to curate specific articles.
