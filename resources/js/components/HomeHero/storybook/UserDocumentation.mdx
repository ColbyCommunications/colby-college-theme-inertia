# Home Hero

The **Home Hero** block creates a large, immersive header designed specifically for the homepage.  
It fills most of the screen and combines a **background image or video with prominent headline content and optional calls to action**.

This block is intended to create a strong first impression when visitors arrive on the site.

---

## When to Use This Block

Use the Home Hero block when:

- You want a visually impactful introduction on the homepage
- You want to highlight a key message or campaign
- You want to feature a background image or video with supporting content

### Example uses:

- Homepage introductions
- Campaign or initiative highlights
- Major announcements
- Seasonal or promotional messaging

## Heading

The **Heading** field contains the main message displayed in the hero.

This text appears prominently over the background media and is typically the first thing visitors see.

Use short, impactful language that clearly communicates the primary message of the page.

---

## Caption

The **Caption** field allows you to add a small line of text that appears near the bottom of the hero.

This can be used to:

- Credit a photographer or source
- Provide context about the image or video
- Add a short descriptive note

---

## Video

The **Video** field allows you to upload a background video for the hero.

When a video is added:

- It plays automatically
- It loops continuously
- It displays without sound

Use video when you want to create a more dynamic visual experience.

If no video is added, the block will display the background image instead.

---

## Image

The **Image** field provides the fallback background image for the hero.

This image is required and will appear when:

- No video is provided
- The browser cannot play the video

Choose images that:

- Work well at very large sizes
- Have strong visual impact
- Maintain readability for the overlay text

---

## Buttons

Optional **buttons** can be added to create calls to action within the hero.

These buttons may link to:

- Key pages
- Featured programs
- Event information
- Campaign landing pages

Limit the number of buttons to maintain a clear and focused message.

---

## Layout Behavior

### Full Screen Hero

The Home Hero fills most of the visible screen when the page loads, creating a large visual introduction.

---

### Background Media

The background displays either:

- A looping **video**, or
- A full-width **image**

Text content is layered over the media with a subtle gradient to improve readability.

---

### Content Placement

The main heading and buttons appear near the lower portion of the hero to maintain visibility against the background media.

---

## Tips for Best Results

- Use **short, powerful headlines** for maximum impact
- Choose **high-quality images or videos** that work at large sizes
- Ensure background media does not make text difficult to read
- Limit calls to action to **one or two buttons**
- Use captions when credit or context is needed
